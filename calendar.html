The Food Flower Market Promise

Why Our Gift Reminder Service Matters

In our busy modern lives, forgetting key milestones is common, but it can impact personal and professional relationships. Our service is designed to add a thoughtful, luxurious touch without requiring hours of planning.

By scheduling your special occasion gifts in advance, you guarantee that a hand-packed exotic fruit box paired with matching fresh florals will arrive exactly on time.

Every delivery includes a custom, calligraphy-written envelope with your message, giving the presentation a deep, personal touch that digital greetings simply cannot duplicate.

How It Works: 3 Simple Steps

  • 1. Log Your Annual Dates

    Enter birthdays, anniversaries, and holidays once. We secure them in your calendar.

  • 2. Receive Soft Notifications

    One week before the event, you get a short reminder email detailing the chosen fruits and floral styles.

  • 3. We Prepare and Deliver

    We select fresh Segundo Frutas mangoes and seasonal stems, handwriting your gift card for delivery.
