Customer Support FAQs

Quick answers to common questions about managing deliveries, corporate accounts, and customized box arrangements.

What are the corporate gifting options and discounts?

We provide custom packages for corporate clients wishing to deliver healthy gift baskets to employees or key stakeholders. For ongoing orders of 10+ boxes per month, we offer a dedicated account manager, custom branded cards, and up to 15% discount. Contact our corporate support division via the form above for quotes.

Can I temporarily pause or change the delivery address for my subscription?

Yes. You can pause, resume, or redirect deliveries through your online customer portal. Changes must be finalized at least 4 days before the scheduled dispatch date to guarantee our team redirects the fresh shipment appropriately.

What zones do your direct fleet vehicles cover?

Our direct, temperature-controlled delivery vans cover Greater London, Essex, and Kent (dispatched from our Barking hub). For areas outside these zones, we use partner express cold-chain couriers to deliver exotic fruit subscription boxes within 24 hours of packing.

What is your refund policy if the fruit is damaged or unsatisfactory?

We guarantee 100% ripeness and freshness. If a fruit arrives damaged or below your expectations, email a photo of the item to customer support within 24 hours of delivery. We will issue a replacement or credit your account for the item immediately.
