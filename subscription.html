Subscription FAQs

Have questions about how our premium fruit delivery and custom box gifting plans work? Find your answers here.

How are the exotic fruits sourced and selected?

We work directly with certified organic and fair-trade orchards globally. Our team inspects every crop of Segundo Frutas mangoes, mangosteen, and granadilla. Only the top 5% of harvested fruit—judged by brix sweetness levels, skin purity, and size—is chosen for our subscriptions.

Can I customize the items or substitute pastries for gluten-free diets?

Absolutely. During checkout or in your member profile, you can request customizations. You can swap out elements (e.g., replace our artisan pastries with gluten-free cronuts or organic honey) and request strict floral color palettes to match the recipient's interior aesthetic.

How does the Gift Reminder Service integrate with my subscription?

When you set up your Gifting Calendar, you can link specific boxes to key dates (such as birthdays or anniversaries). Instead of a standard monthly box arriving on a random date, we automatically adjust your shipping cycle so that the box arrives exactly on the day of the special occasion.

What happens if a fruit or flower variety is out of season?

Due to natural crop seasons, box contents shift. However, we guarantee that any replacement fruit or flower will match or exceed the premium value of the scheduled item. We will always notify you ahead of shipment with a list of ingredients in your box.

How do you guarantee freshness during delivery?

We ship all healthy gift baskets in custom insulated cases with reusable non-toxic ice gel pads. This maintains a steady 8°C environment. Furthermore, local deliveries in London are handled by our dedicated fleet, bypassing regular postal routes to ensure next-day arrival.
